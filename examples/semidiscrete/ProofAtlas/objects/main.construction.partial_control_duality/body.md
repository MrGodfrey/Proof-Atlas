Let $\mathcal{U}$ be the subspace of $L^1_{\mathbb{F}}(0,T;L^2(\Omega;E_j))$ generated by observed traces of solutions to [[main.model.backward_adjoint_system]]:

$$
\mathcal{U}\triangleq
\left\{
\chi_{G_0}z
\mid
z \text{ solves the backward adjoint system}
\text{ with }
\eta\in L^2_{\mathcal{F}_T}(\Omega;E_j)
\right\}.
$$

The duality construction defines a bounded linear functional $\mathcal{L}$ on $\mathcal{U}$ by

$$
\mathcal{L}(\chi_{G_0}z)
=
-\mathbb{E}\int_{\mathcal{M}}y_0 z(0),
\qquad
\forall\,z\in\mathcal{U}.
$$

It extends to a bounded linear functional on

$$
L^1_{\mathbb{F}}(0,T;L^2(\Omega;E_j)).
$$

Consequently, by the Riesz representation theorem cited as Theorem 2.55 of [[source.lue_zhang_2021]], there exists

$$
u\in L^\infty_{\mathbb{F}}(0,T;L^2(\Omega;E_j)).
$$

This adapted control satisfies the representation identity [[main.statement.partial_control_representation]] and the control estimate in [[main.claim.partial_null_control]].
