This is recalled from [[source.boyer_2010a]].

There exist constants $C_1,C_4>0$ and $h_1>0$ such that for all $0<h\leq h_1$ and

$$
0<\mu\leq \frac{C_1}{h^2},
$$

we have

$$
\sum_{\mu_k\leq \mu}|\alpha_k|^2
=
\int_{\mathcal{M}}
\left|
\sum_{\mu_k\leq \mu}\alpha_k\phi_k
\right|^2
\leq
C_4 e^{C_4\sqrt{\mu}}
\int_{\mathcal{M}}
\left|
\sum_{\mu_k\leq \mu}\alpha_k\phi_k
\right|^2
$$

for every choice of the coefficients $\{\alpha_k\}_{k=1}^{|\mathcal{M}|}$ with $\alpha_k\in\mathbb{C}$.

This imported estimate is the low-frequency input for [[main.claim.observability]].
