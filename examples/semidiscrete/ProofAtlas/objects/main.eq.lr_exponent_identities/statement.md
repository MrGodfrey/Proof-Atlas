The product in [[main.eq.lr_induction_product]] is estimated using

$$
C_5^{j_0+2}=e^{(j_0+2)\ln 2},
$$

$$
\prod_{j=0}^{j_0+1}T_j^{-2}
=
e^{-2(j_0+2)\ln K-\rho\ln 2\,(j_0+1)(j_0+2)/2},
$$

and

$$
\begin{aligned}
&\sum_{j=0}^{j_0+1}
C_4 2^j-(2^{2j+1}-3\tau)T_j\\
&=
C_4(2^{j_0+2}-1)
+3\tau T/4
-\frac{2K(2^{(2-\rho)(j_0+2)}-1)}{2^{2-\rho}-1}.
\end{aligned}
$$
