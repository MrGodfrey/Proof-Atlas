Author: Qi Lu.

Title: Some results on the controllability of forward stochastic heat equations with control on the drift.

Journal: Journal of Functional Analysis, 260(3), 832--851, 2011.

DOI: `10.1016/j.jfa.2010.10.018`.

Role in this paper: cited for null controllability of stochastic parabolic equations with only one control on the drift, and for the continuous problem [[main.model.continuous_problem]] on general domains, diffusion tensors, and boundary conditions.
