Title:

**Null controllability for semi-discrete stochastic parabolic equations with control on the drift**

## Abstract

This paper investigates the null controllability for a class of semi-discrete stochastic parabolic equations in arbitrary dimensions with only one control acting on the drift term. The spatial variable is discretized by a finite difference scheme, while the time variable remains continuous. By an observability estimate for partial sums of eigenfunctions of the discrete elliptic operator and employing a time iteration method, we prove the null controllability of the system.

## Keywords

Stochastic parabolic equation, null controllability, observability.
